#+TITLE: Story Canon - Example
#+AUTHOR: Polymuse Example
#+DATE: 2025-01-09

This is an example canon file for prose writing. It demonstrates how to
track characters, locations, and other story elements for consistency.

* Characters

** [Characters] Sarah Chen
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: sarah-chen
:Age: 28
:Role: protagonist
:END:

*** Description
A brilliant cybersecurity researcher working at a major tech company in San
Francisco. She has a knack for finding vulnerabilities in systems that
everyone else thinks are secure.

*** Appearance
- Height: 5'6"
- Build: Athletic
- Hair: Long black hair, usually in a ponytail
- Eyes: Dark brown
- Notable features: Small scar above left eyebrow from a childhood accident

*** Background
Born in Seattle to immigrant parents who ran a small restaurant. Got into
programming as a teenager and never looked back. Has a younger brother she's
very protective of.

*** Personality
Analytical and methodical in her work, but tends to act impulsively in her
personal life. Fiercely loyal to friends. Has a dry sense of humor that
catches people off guard.

** [Characters] Marcus Reynolds
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: marcus-reynolds
:Age: 45
:Role: antagonist
:END:

*** Description
Former government intelligence analyst turned corporate spy. Works for
various clients to steal trade secrets and compromising information.

*** Appearance
- Height: 6'1"
- Build: Lean
- Hair: Salt and pepper, well-groomed
- Eyes: Steel gray
- Notable features: Always wears expensive suits

*** Background
Spent 15 years in military intelligence before becoming disillusioned with
government work. Realized his skills were more profitable in the private
sector.

*** Personality
Charismatic and manipulative. Excellent at reading people and finding their
weaknesses. Has a code of honor, but it's entirely his own.

* Locations

** [Locations] CyberGuard HQ
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: cyberguard-hq
:City: San Francisco
:END:

*** Description
A gleaming 12-story glass and steel building in the Financial District.
CyberGuard is one of the leading cybersecurity firms in Silicon Valley.

*** Details
- Security: State-of-the-art biometric access, security guards at every entrance
- Layout: Open floor plan on most floors, executive offices on the top two floors
- Notable areas: The "War Room" on floor 8 where the security operations center runs 24/7
- Atmosphere: Modern and sleek, but high-pressure

** [Locations] The Cipher
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: the-cipher
:City: San Francisco
:END:

*** Description
A hacker-friendly coffee shop in the Mission District. Popular hangout for
security researchers and programmers.

*** Details
- Ambiance: Dim lighting, mismatched furniture, walls covered with tech stickers
- Special features: Private booths with VPN-secured connections, white noise generators
- Owner: Former hacker known only as "Ghost"
- Notable: Meeting place for the underground security community

* Events

** [Events] The DataCore Breach
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: datacore-breach
:Date: 2024-03-15
:END:

*** Description
A major data breach at DataCore Systems that exposed 50 million user records.
This event triggered Sarah's investigation into a larger conspiracy.

*** Details
- Initial discovery: Sarah noticed unusual patterns in network traffic
- Impact: Led to congressional hearings and massive fines
- Connection: Later discovered to be orchestrated by Marcus Reynolds
- Outcome: This breach put Sarah on Marcus's radar

* Technology

** [Technology] Phantom Protocol
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: phantom-protocol
:Status: fictional
:END:

*** Description
A sophisticated encryption protocol that can hide data streams within
legitimate network traffic, making them nearly impossible to detect.

*** Technical Details
- Uses steganography to embed encrypted data
- Self-destructs if tampering is detected
- Requires specialized hardware to decode
- Vulnerability: Leaves a subtle timing signature that Sarah discovered
